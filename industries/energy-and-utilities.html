Build the Digital Backbone of Modern Utilities

Acesoft empowers energy and utility providers with digital platforms designed for resilience, regulatory compliance, and operational efficiency. From modernizing legacy systems to digitizing field operations and unifying critical data across electricity, gas, and water networks — we engineer infrastructure that supports predictive maintenance, grid innovation, and customer transparency, all without adding complexity to existing systems.


Powering the Shift: How Technology is Reimagining Energy & Utilities

Today's utility operators face unprecedented challenges — growing load demand, sustainability mandates, fragmented legacy systems, and aging infrastructure. Outdated SCADA tools, manual workflows, and siloed asset monitoring continue to hinder agility, scalability, and service quality. Acesoft helps utilities overcome these limitations by designing real-time operational platforms that increase grid visibility, uptime, and digital innovation. Our transformation strategy is built in collaboration with field managers, engineers, and customer teams to ensure essential services remain uninterrupted during modernization.


Our Core Capabilities:

  • Replacing paper-based workflows with live dashboards and automated task flows
  • Streamlining SCADA, AMI, and GIS data into single-source-of-truth environments
  • Automating risk alerts, outage reporting, and maintenance schedules
  • Delivering grid optimization tools that support DERs, renewables, and hybrid energy models
  • Enabling predictive analytics that support load balancing and compliance

Our goal isn't to overlay software — it's to build the digital foundation that supports real-time control, remote operations, and long-term scalability across the grid.
